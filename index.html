About Me

My Name is Mario Nyangintaka. I am from A small Township in the East of Johannesburg called Tembisa. I started coding in October 13th 2019. After IT Varsity an Organization based in South Africa, gave me the opportunity to learn. This Organization enables Students to learn code in IT (National-Higher Certificate-Systems Development). I have learned a lot since my commencement.

I have grown to love Code and have realized that Information Technology is more than the code itself. There's a greater purpose attached to it. Problem solving, discipline, time management mixed with creativity, Innovation, tailor-making systems to best suit the specifications of our clients, and in that way we build prolific Brands and great business relationships.

Coding is Fun in so many ways. When I hit a wall, I find a feasible solution. This inturn broadens my knowledge and enables me to forge ahead. I see no other activity I'd rather do that is more constructive than developing a system bit-by-bit and watching it come to fruition. Coding is hard work but with passion it is all a bliss. I aim to become a tech-lead one day.

I can never stop learning in this field, passion just drives me.
